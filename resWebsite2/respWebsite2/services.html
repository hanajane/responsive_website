PRE-PURCHASE INSPECTION

The pre-purchase inspection, aims to buy knowingly and to negotiate well-equipped! It allows you to plan the repairs required if the purchase is made. This inspection identifies visually apparent irregularities arising from normal degradation or construction defects. It includes a comprehensive written report describing the condition of the principal components of the property at the time of the transaction. An indispensable document in case of problems when taking possession.

    Why ?
  • To buy in full knowledge of the facts.
  • To negotiate well-equipped.
  • To plan improvements.
